# 🎯 [Mutation Feed] Lead Gen Automation

### 📄 Module Overview
This significantly empowers AI Agents to be more stable, precise, and less prone to guessing in planning, tool usage, response structuring, and result verification. This feed pack (Mutation Feed) serves precisely to inject a **high-precision lead-generation pipeline** into your workspace. By deploying a "Guarded Decision Loop," it compels AI Agents to endure strict profile triangulation (Inspect First) and sanitization scrubs (Verify) prior to embarking on aggressive scraping—summarily eliminating the "spam-trap" databases inflicted by undisciplined data-harvesting.

### ⚙️ Skill Synergy
- **Recommended Skills**: `search_web`, `read_url_content`, `write_to_file`, `email_dispatch`
- **Synergy Effect**: Mechanically links lead discovery to automated writes. Every contact dispatched into a database is rigorously surveilled by the loop's `Validation` branch. If legitimate contact channels are absent, it forces a localized retry or purges the dead record entirely.

### 🚀 Behavior Upgrade Target
1. **Conditional Branches**: When target sources lack substantive job titles, verifiable emails, or decision-making authorities, it forcibly pivots into the Failure or Clarification branches instead of inventing "ghost leads."
2. **Mandatory Verify**: Prior to culminating lists (CSV/JSON), forcibly audits whether the captured demographic strictly satisfies the user's pre-defined ICP (Ideal Customer Profile).
3. **Check Tooling**: Principally confirms whether the search utilities can penetrate the target platforms (e.g., LinkedIn public profiles) or 3rd-party Data APIs without triggering rate limits.
4. **Pervasive Scope**: Perpetually imposes these laws during non-trivial tasks involving Business Development (BD), lead funneling, and contact aggregation.
