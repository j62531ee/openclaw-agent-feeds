# 📊 [Mutation Feed] Market Research Auto

### 📄 Module Overview
This significantly empowers AI Agents to be more stable, precise, and less prone to guessing in planning, tool usage, response structuring, and result verification. This feed pack (Mutation Feed) acts as your apex apparatus for **executing high-precision economic trend sweeps and sentiment arrays**. It upgrades your workspace into a calculating field researcher. Fused with a "Guarded Decision Loop," it ruthlessly compels AI Agents to trigger multi-source triangulations (Inspect First) and cull extreme noise (Verify) prior to drafting intelligence. This surgically prevents the "Echo Chamber Effect" caused by agents regurgitating lone PR releases into worthless overviews.

### ⚙️ Skill Synergy
- **Recommended Skills**: `search_web`, `read_url_content`, `write_to_file`
- **Synergy Effect**: It cages the wild capabilities of broad network searches inside an ironclad intel-extraction framework. Post-search, data authority is interrogated by the `Validation` branch. If a query hauls in low-tier content farms, the search lexicon is forcibly ruptured and recalibrated.

### 🚀 Behavior Upgrade Target
1. **Conditional Branches**: Deprived of empirical metrics (e.g., CAGR, TAM) within a targeted niche, the AI triggers the Clarification Branch to broaden the hunting azimuth, unconditionally refusing to forge synthetic metrics.
2. **Mandatory Verify**: Prior to sculpting the macroscopic trend map, it washes and eliminates "emotional but analytically sterile" troll outbursts, distilling only business-critical consumer pain points.
3. **Check Tooling**: Conducts pre-flight reconnaissance on whether the designated Search tool can vault localization barriers or regional blockades (e.g., researching Japan-centric domains).
4. **Pervasive Scope**: Perpetually imposes these directives during non-trivial tasks mapping market scaling, sentiment trajectory tracking, and emergent trend scanning.

- **Recommended**: Gemini 3.0 Pro / GPT-5.3 / Claude Sonnet 4.6
- **Minimum**: Gemini 3.1 Flash / GPT-5.1
